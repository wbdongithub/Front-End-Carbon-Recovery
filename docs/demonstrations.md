# Demonstration Program

FECR has two complementary demonstration tools.

## Transparent microscreen model

The see-through model helps plant staff, engineers and decision-makers understand belt filtration, fiber capture and the importance of mechanical dewatering.

![Transparent microscreen model](/img/microscreen-model-b.png)

## Portable trailer-mounted unit

The field unit can be brought to a wastewater treatment plant and connected at candidate process locations.

![Portable demonstration trailer](/img/demo-trailer.jpg)

A demonstration program can document:

- influent and effluent TSS;
- fiber capture;
- cake solids concentration;
- hydraulic capacity;
- polymer needs, if any;
- operating stability;
- practical installation issues;
- effects of testing different process locations.

## Purpose

The demonstration is not merely a sales presentation. It is a way to reduce uncertainty and develop a plant-specific basis for design.
