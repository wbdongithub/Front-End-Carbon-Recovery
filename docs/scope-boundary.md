# Where This Knowledge Base Stops

The FECR Knowledge Base covers:

- Front-end solids recovery
- Microscreens and RBFs
- Mechanical dewatering
- Low-temperature drying
- Pyrolysis
- Production of biochar
- Plant-wide implementation and benefits

It does **not** attempt to cover the full science and engineering of using biochar for wastewater cleanup. Topics such as activation, PFAS adsorption, nZVI modification, electrochemical treatment, arsenic removal, and contaminant-specific media design will be developed in a separate Biochar Knowledge Base.

This boundary keeps FECR practical and focused on the immediate wastewater-plant problem: remove solids early, reduce downstream burdens, and create a manageable final product.
