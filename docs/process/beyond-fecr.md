# Beyond FECR

FECR ends with the production of biochar and recovered energy. What happens next is important, but it quickly becomes a separate subject.

Biochar may have applications in:

- Carbon sequestration
- Soil improvement
- Wastewater polishing
- PFAS adsorption
- Metals removal
- Engineered media
- nZVI-modified biochar
- Electrochemical treatment systems

Those topics belong in a future companion **Biochar Knowledge Base**.

The FECR site should stay focused on front-end solids recovery, drying, and pyrolysis in wastewater treatment plants.
