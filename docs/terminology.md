# Terminology

| Preferred FECR term | Related terms in literature | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Microscreen | Rotating Belt Filter (RBF), Rotating Belt Sieve (RBS) | Continuous belt device for removing suspended solids from wastewater |
| Recovered front-end solids | Cellulosic screenings, filter primary sludge, primary cellulosic sludge | Solids captured before biological treatment |
| Waste activated sludge | WAS, secondary sludge | Biological solids produced in the treatment process |
| Dewatered cake | Pressed solids | Solids after mechanical water removal |
| Low-temperature dryer | Heat-pump sludge dryer, dehumidification dryer | Closed-loop thermal drying system |
| Pyrolysis | Carbonization, thermochemical conversion | Heating with little or no oxygen to form char and gases |
| FECR | Front End Carbon Recovery | The integrated strategy described by this site |

Consistent terminology helps readers move between plain-language explanations and scientific papers that use different names for similar processes.
