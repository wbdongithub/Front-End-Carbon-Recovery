# Terminology

| Preferred FECR term | Related terms in literature | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| **Microscreen** | Rotating Belt Filter (RBF), Rotating Belt Sieve (RBS) | Continuous fine-mesh belt used to capture influent solids. |
| **Recovered front-end solids** | Cellulosic screenings, filter primary sludge, primary cellulosic sludge | Solids captured before biological treatment. |
| **Waste activated sludge** | WAS, biological sludge | Biomass produced by the biological treatment process. |
| **Dewatered cake** | Pressed solids, sludge cake | Solids after mechanical removal of free water. |
| **FECR** | Front End Carbon Recovery | The integrated strategy of early solids recovery and staged processing. |

The site will normally use **microscreen** in explanatory text and **RBF** when referring to scientific literature that uses that term.
