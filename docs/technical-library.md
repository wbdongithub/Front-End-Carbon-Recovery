# Technical Library

The library will grow as documents are reviewed and summarized. It will be organized by subject:

## Microscreens and primary filtration

Research on rotating belt filters, rotating belt sieves, particle capture, COD recovery and effects on downstream biological treatment.

## Cellulose in wastewater

Sources, fate, recovery rates, dewaterability and potential uses of recovered cellulose-rich solids.

## Plant impacts

Aeration, nitrogen removal, sludge production, digestion, clarification and practical plant capacity.

## Dewatering and drying

Mechanical dewatering, cake solids, heat-pump drying, energy use and transport implications.

## Pyrolysis and contaminant destruction

Feed preparation, operating temperature, gas combustion, emissions, mass balances and biochar yield.

## Demonstration and case-study documents

Pilot plans, sampling protocols, photos, presentations and performance summaries.

> This prototype does not yet publish the uploaded source papers. Copyright and permission status will be checked before documents or vendor images are placed on the public site.
